Site development & preparation for Middle Tennessee projects

Advanced Site Development, the hexa Excavating Way

hexa Excavating specializes in comprehensive site development, self-performing all earthmoving, grading, and infrastructure preparation to transform raw land into construction-ready sites.

Our teams add value by pairing strategic survey and planning with detailed ground-level expertise. Committed to value engineering and leveraging advanced technologies like 3D site modeling, our skilled crews ensure precision and reliability on every project.

Proactive Solutions for Site Preparation Challenges

Delays, schedule interruptions, and cost overruns can quickly turn a dream project into a nightmare. At hexa Excavating, we help prevent these issues by providing accurate estimates, extensive knowledge of the physical landscape, and access to the full range of machinery and equipment needed for exceptional site preparation.

What Sets hexa Excavating’s Site Development ServicesApart

We offer our clients the best of both worlds: old-school reliability and quality workmanship paired with the latest advanced technologies and tools. The result is projects with solid foundations that serve today’s and tomorrow’s needs.

Individualized Plans for a Range of Clients

No two clients are alike. Whether we’re working DOTs or developers, we craft a personalized approach to site preparation, ensuring all goals are met and the project is a success. We work with a number of client types, including:

01 Value Engineering

Even when budgets are set and estimates are in, we’re able to analyze and refine processes in creative ways to maximize time and control costs without sacrificing quality.

02 Local Engineering

As Middle Tennessee’s excavating provider of choice for decades, we’re constantly staying in the loop on local engineering requirements to keep projects built in compliance.

03 Strategic Project Planning

We can help ease any potential roadblocks with a proactive approach that takes care of any needs that help in areas such as material facilitation and utility planning.

04 Permitting Process

Vital for the success of projects but sometimes difficult to navigate, we’ll help you get the permits you need to get to work by driving the process for you with the right organizations.

05 Platting Process

We’ll help you work through any phases of your project that call for lot size and parceling considerations, including combing through survey information and matching that with local requirements.

06 As-Built Requirement Process

When as-built requirements are in place, we can help translate the end result that’s needed into an actionable, easy-to-follow process to get you to “done.”
