The Start of Hexa Excavating

Hexa Excavating began as a small, family-owned, and locally operated site contracting company with a big dream and a strong work ethic. From day one, our mission was simple — deliver honest, high-quality excavation services that people could trust.

Built on Hard Work and Trust

In our early days, we relied entirely on word of mouth, and it quickly became the foundation of our success. Through dedication, reliable service, and strong relationships, our reputation grew within the community.

Locally Grown, Community Trusted

As the years passed, Hexa Excavating expanded its services, team, and equipment — but never lost the personal touch that started it all. Today, we continue to serve with the same values we were built on: integrity, quality, and community focus.

Our Vision

To enhance the potential of our people, our projects, and the soil we dig.

Our Mission

To add value to the lives of those we serve — to be the experts our partners trust, the leaders our communities deserve, and the providers our families depend on.
